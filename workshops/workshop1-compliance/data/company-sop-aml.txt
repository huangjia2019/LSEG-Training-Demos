LSEG INTERNAL — STANDARD OPERATING PROCEDURE
ANTI-MONEY LAUNDERING (AML) COMPLIANCE

Last Updated: January 2026

1. CUSTOMER ONBOARDING

1.1 All new customers must undergo standard CDD before account activation.
1.2 CDD verification threshold: SGD 20,000 (per MAS Notice 648).
1.3 EDD is required for customers identified as PEPs.
1.4 Onboarding approval chain:
    - Standard customers: Compliance Analyst → Team Lead
    - PEPs: Compliance Analyst → Team Lead → Head of Compliance

2. TRANSACTION MONITORING

2.1 Daily batch screening of all transactions against sanctions lists.
2.2 Manual review of transactions exceeding SGD 50,000.
2.3 STR filing deadline: 15 business days from detection.
2.4 Current monitoring tool: Manual spreadsheet + quarterly batch analysis.

3. RECORD KEEPING

3.1 CDD records retained for 5 years post-relationship termination.
3.2 Transaction records retained for 5 years.
3.3 Storage: Secure internal file server with access logging.

4. TRAINING

4.1 Annual AML awareness training for all staff.
4.2 Quarterly updates for Compliance team on regulatory changes.
